Privacy Policy (English)

Last updated: February 2026

PhiStoic is a minimalist Stoic quote app developed by Martin K. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data — or rather, why there is very little to explain.

Data Collection

PhiStoic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require an account, does not use analytics, and does not connect to any external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

PhiStoic stores the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • The start date of your 365-day journey
  • Whether you have seen the welcome screen
  • Your theme preference (dark or light)
  • The IDs of your saved favorite quotes

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

PhiStoic does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

Permissions

PhiStoic requests only the permissions necessary to display home screen widgets and schedule daily notifications. No other permissions are required.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach the developer at: phistudioone@gmail.com

PhiStoic es una aplicación minimalista de citas estoicas desarrollada por Martin K. Esta política de privacidad explica cómo la app gestiona tus datos — o más bien, por qué hay muy poco que explicar.
